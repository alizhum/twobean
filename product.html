description

Hey there! This is Max & Alex on the line, the brains behind Twobean.coffee. We’ve been hands-on from roasting these beans to grinding them up and packing in these cool nitrogen-filled drip bags. Why? So you can kick back with a top-notch cup of coffee wherever, whenever. All you need is one of these drip bags and some boiling water, and boom – you’re tasting coffee like never before. Yep, that’s our jam here. Catering to diverse tastes, yet united by a love for coffee.

Our twist on the world’s oldest coffee blend, Mocha Java, spiced up by authentic beans from the estates of East Java, the Harrar and the Amaro region of southwestern Ethiopia. We roast ’em just right, between the first and second cracks, to get this awesome brandy tone and a gentle kick of tropical fruits and berries acidity in flavor.

recommendation

How to store coffee to keep it fresh?

To keep your coffee as fresh as possible, we recommend storing it in an airtight container at room temperature. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight, excessive heat, or moisture, as these can affect the coffee’s flavor and freshness. Ideally, coffee should be consumed within a month of opening to enjoy its peak flavor. If you’ve purchased coffee in bulk, consider dividing it into smaller portions and only opening what you need.
